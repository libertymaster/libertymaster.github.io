Marc Bloch: A Man of Courage

10 Mar 2015 - Lamont Michael Kimberlin

The foreign policy of the United States has not only produced death, starvation, and increased chaos around the world; but it has also created a system by which irrational and irresponsible programs are carried out in the name of spreading democracy (Johnson). For example, hundreds of pounds of one-hundred dollar bills were sent to Iraq and distributed by American soldiers to Iraqi fighters, including fighters partnered or associated with al-Qaeda (Jasper). American foreign policy architects, including those elected and appointed, must put an end to the bribes, corrupt activity, and secret operations which shape U.S. foreign policy, and directly contribute to the deaths and injuries of hundreds of thousands of American soldiers and innocent civilians, while endangering the national security of America.
