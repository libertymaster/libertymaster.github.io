5 Feb 2025 - Lamont Michael Kimberlin

According to Merriam-Webster Dictionary:

1 tale, story

2 a chronological record of significant events (such as those affecting a nation or institution) often including an explanation of their causes (a history of Japan)

c an account of a patient's medical background reviewing her medical history

d an established record a prisoner with a history of violence
